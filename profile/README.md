This repository, alongside [a](https://github.com/MuchAEDu/AEdu2026) [few](https://github.com/MuchAEDu/awesome_aedu) others, are my notes on teaching with and about artificial life topics. 
<br>

<strong>We are planning a virtual workshop</strong>: The 2026 AEdu Artificial Life Education workshop (AEdu) will be held online on May 18 and 19 (western time zones) and (if enough participants register) on May 25 and 26 for eastern time zones (centered on Japan Standard Time). The official page is [https://alife-edu.github.io/aedu-virtual-workshop-2026.html](https://alife-edu.github.io/aedu-virtual-workshop-2026.html), and you can register for the event [here](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeQ-ngpkBxr58mm8rGCKmU9Dna8DVHER3gXP8aZ6n0YpuO77A/viewform).
